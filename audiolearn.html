What You'll Learn

Level 1: The Physics of Sound - Start with the fundamentals. Understand what sound is, how it travels, and the basic properties that affect everything in audio production.

Level 2: Digital Audio Basics - Bridge the gap between analog and digital. Learn about sample rates, bit depth, and how modern audio systems work.

Level 3: Microphones - Master the most important tool in audio. Learn about different microphone types, polar patterns, and proper placement techniques.

Level 4: Mixing Basics - Get hands-on with mixers. Understand gain structure, EQ, compression, and the fundamentals of creating a great mix.

Level 5: Live Sound Production - Take your skills to the stage. Learn PA system setup, monitor management, feedback elimination, and running a smooth show.

Level 6: Church Audio Specialist - Specialized training for worship environments. Learn worship sound mixing, pastor mic techniques, live streaming audio, and volunteer training.
