========================================
EMAIL NOTIFICATION SETUP GUIDE
For Ev1media Booking System
========================================

Your booking system now sends emails automatically when someone submits a booking!
Follow these steps to activate email notifications:

========================================
STEP 1: Create EmailJS Account (FREE)
========================================

1. Go to: https://www.emailjs.com/
2. Click "Sign Up" (top right)
3. Create a free account with your email
4. Confirm your email address

========================================
STEP 2: Add Email Service
========================================

1. After logging in, go to "Email Services" (left sidebar)
2. Click "Add New Service"
3. Choose your email provider:
   - Gmail (recommended if you have Gmail)
   - Outlook
   - Yahoo
   - Or use "Personal Email Server"

4. For Gmail:
   - Click "Connect Account"
   - Select your Gmail account (info@ev1media.com or your business email)
   - Allow EmailJS to send emails on your behalf
   
5. Copy the "Service ID" (looks like: service_abc1234)
   - You'll need this later!

========================================

========================================
STEP 4: Get Your Public Key
========================================

1. Go to "Account" (top right corner)
2. Click "General" tab
3. Find "Public Key" section
4. Copy your Public Key (looks like: abc123XYZ456)

========================================
STEP 5: Update Your Website Files
========================================

Open this file: booking.html

Find these lines:
   emailjs.init("YOUR_PUBLIC_KEY");

Replace with:
   emailjs.init("YOUR_ACTUAL_PUBLIC_KEY_HERE");

-----------------------------------

Open this file: booking.js

Find this line:
   emailjs.send('YOUR_SERVICE_ID', 'YOUR_TEMPLATE_ID', emailParams)

Replace with:
   emailjs.send('service_abc1234', 'template_xyz5678', emailParams)
   (Use your actual Service ID and Template ID)

========================================
STEP 6: Test Your Setup
========================================

1. Save all files
2. Open your website
3. Go to the booking page
4. Fill out a test booking
5. Submit the form
