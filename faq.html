Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our AV solutions and digital marketing services

AV Solutions & Equipment Rental

How much does it cost to rent a sound system in Georgia or Florida?

Our sound system rental packages range from $500 to $2,000+ depending on the size of your event and equipment needed. All packages include a professional mixer and 2 microphones. We offer customized quotes based on your specific venue size, guest count, and audio requirements. Contact us for a free consultation and detailed pricing for your event.

What's included in your sound system rental packages?

Every sound system rental package includes a professional-grade digital mixer and 2 wireless microphones as standard. Additional equipment varies by package but can include professional PA speakers, line array systems, additional microphones, monitor systems, and subwoofers. We also offer add-on services like DJ services and technical support. Setup and pickup are included in all rentals.

Do you provide setup and technical support for events?

Yes! Professional setup and pickup are included with all our AV rentals. For larger events or complex productions, we offer on-site technical support services where our experienced technicians stay throughout your event to manage audio, troubleshoot any issues, and ensure everything runs smoothly. This is included in our Live Event Production package or available as an add-on to standard rentals.

How far in advance should I book your AV services?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance for most events to ensure equipment availability. For large events, weddings, or peak season dates (holidays and summer months), booking 6-8 weeks ahead is ideal. However, we understand last-minute needs arise - contact us even if your event is coming up soon, as we'll do our best to accommodate your timeline.

What types of events do you specialize in?

We specialize in a wide range of events including church services, corporate events, conferences, weddings, concerts, festivals, and community gatherings. Our team has extensive experience with both indoor and outdoor venues of all sizes. We're particularly experienced in church AV systems and have worked with numerous congregations throughout Georgia and Florida to enhance their worship services and special events.

Service Areas & Availability

What areas in Georgia and Florida do you serve?

Ev1media provides AV solutions and digital marketing services throughout Georgia and Florida. We frequently serve major cities and surrounding areas in both states. Contact us to confirm service availability for your specific location - we're happy to travel for events of all sizes.

Do you travel outside of Georgia and Florida?

While we primarily serve Georgia and Florida, we're open to traveling for larger events or special circumstances. Travel fees may apply for locations outside our standard service area. Contact us to discuss your event location and we'll provide a customized quote.

Digital Marketing Services

How much does social media management cost?

Our social media management services range from $300 to $1,500 per month depending on the number of platforms, posting frequency, and services included. We offer flexible payment plans to make our services accessible for small businesses and churches. All packages include content creation, posting schedules, community engagement, and monthly analytics reports. Contact us for a customized quote based on your specific needs and goals.

Can you help with both AV and marketing services together?

Absolutely! One of our unique strengths is offering integrated AV and digital marketing solutions. We can provide sound systems and event production for your live events while also capturing content, managing your social media, and promoting your events online. This integrated approach ensures consistent messaging and maximum impact across both your physical and digital presence. Many of our church and business clients benefit from this comprehensive service model.

How long does it take to build a website?

Most website projects take 2-4 weeks from initial consultation to launch, depending on the complexity and features required. Simple business websites with a few pages can be completed in about 2 weeks, while more complex sites with e-commerce, custom features, or extensive content may take 4-6 weeks. We work closely with you throughout the process to ensure the final product meets your needs and timeline.

Payment & Policies

Do you offer payment plans or financing options?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for our digital marketing services to make them accessible for small businesses and churches with limited budgets. For AV equipment rentals and event production, we typically require a deposit to secure your date with the balance due before or on the event date. Contact us to discuss payment options that work best for your situation.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule my event?

We understand that plans change. Our cancellation and rescheduling policies vary depending on how close to the event date you need to make changes. Generally, if you cancel or reschedule with sufficient notice (typically 2+ weeks), we can often accommodate changes with minimal fees. Contact us as soon as possible if your plans change, and we'll work with you to find the best solution.
