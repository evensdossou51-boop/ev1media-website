FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

How big of an audience can the sound rental cover?
Typically up to ~120 people indoors for speech‑focused events. For music or larger rooms, ask about adding a sub or extra speakers.
Do you stay during the event?
For most small events we set up, sound‑check, and provide a brief tutorial. Operator service can be added if you prefer full hands‑off.
What does AV restoration include for churches?
We audit your full signal path, tidy and label wiring, set proper gain structure, tune the system, and train your volunteers with a cheat sheet.
Can you stream to both YouTube and Facebook?
Yes; we can set you up to simulcast using simple software or a hardware encoder, depending on your needs.
