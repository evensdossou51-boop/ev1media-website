About Ev1media - Audio, Networking, and IT Services

Ev1media serves churches, businesses, and events across Florida. We focus on reliable audio systems, networking infrastructure, and practical IT support that keep your operations running smoothly.

Our Founder: Evens Dossou

I'm Evens Dossou, the founder, and my journey in the technical world began with a deep passion for the Audio-Visual (AV) industry, particularly Audio.

Professional AV Experience Since 2010

I started working hands-on in the professional audio and visual industry in 2010, gaining extensive experience in sound reinforcement, live event production, and church AV systems. This experience led me to build Ev1media, a company focused on providing industry-leading audiovisual support and sound system rentals, initially specializing in complex AV needs for churches in Florida, while also driving a new standard of excellence across the wider event production sector.

Education & Expertise

To ensure we deliver cutting-edge service, I am continually advancing my skills by pursuing an Audio Production degree, AVIXA certification (for AV industry standards), and a Business Administration certificate.
