Our Vision

Our goal is to become a trusted, faith-driven leader in technology, media and business ventures across Florida and beyond, delivering reliable workplace AI, business software, digital platforms, professional audio, video, livestream, projection, IT and networking solutions that empower churches, businesses and organizations to work, communicate, grow and serve with excellence, impact and purpose.

From integration work to a broader technology vision.

Founded by Evens Dossou, EV1 Media began with professional audiovisual and technical integration. That foundation has expanded into a broader vision: creating and operating technology companies that help organizations communicate better, access information and work more efficiently.

Leadership

Evens Dossou · Founder, EV1 Media

Evens Dossou is the founder of EV1 Media. His journey in technology began with a deep passion for audio, media and the systems that help churches and organizations communicate clearly and operate with confidence.

Since 2010, Evens has worked hands-on in the professional audio and visual industry, gaining experience in sound reinforcement, live event production, church AV systems and technical integration. That practical foundation shaped EV1 Media and now supports its broader focus on business operations, workplace AI and software ventures.

To continue delivering dependable results, he is advancing his skills through audio production studies, AVIXA certification for AV industry standards and business administration training. He is also developing expertise in programming, artificial intelligence, web and software development, and project management. His approach is rooted in practical problem-solving, clear communication and building systems organizations can depend on.
