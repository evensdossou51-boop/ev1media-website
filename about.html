Our Vision

I'm Evens Dossou, the founder of EV1Media. My journey in the technical world began with a deep passion for audio, media, and the systems that help churches communicate clearly and serve with excellence.

Professional AV Experience Since 2010

I started working hands-on in the professional audio and visual industry in 2010, gaining experience in sound reinforcement, live event production, and church AV systems. That experience shaped EV1Media into a company focused on helping churches and organizations renovate, integrate, and operate technical systems with confidence.

Education & Expertise
