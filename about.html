About Ev1media - Professional AV & Marketing Services

Ev1media is a dynamic solutions provider serving churches and businesses throughout Georgia and Florida since 2022. We stand at the intersection of professional Audio-Visual production and essential digital marketing technology, delivering comprehensive event production and digital marketing services to help you succeed.

Our Founder: Evens Dossou

I'm Evens Dossou, the founder, and my journey in the technical world began with a deep passion for the Audio-Visual (AV) industry, particularly Audio.

Professional AV Experience Since 2010

I started working hands-on in the professional audio and visual industry in 2010, gaining extensive experience in sound reinforcement, live event production, and church AV systems. This experience led me to build Ev1media, a company focused on providing industry-leading audiovisual support and sound system rentals, initially specializing in complex AV needs for churches in Georgia and Florida, while also driving a new standard of excellence across the wider event production sector.

Education & Expertise

To ensure we deliver cutting-edge service, I am continually advancing my skills by pursuing an Audio Production degree, AVIXA certification (for AV industry standards), and a Business Administration certificate.
